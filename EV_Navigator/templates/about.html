About EV Navigator

EV Navigator is a project developed under Prof. Ravi Gupta at IIT Jodhpur. It plans efficient EV routes considering battery and charging stations.

Current implementation: 1 km per 2% battery.

Future: ML model to auto-adjust mileage per vehicle type using supervised learning.
